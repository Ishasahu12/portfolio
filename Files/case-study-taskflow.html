TaskFlow

A task management app designed to help remote teams stay organized, focused, and productive without the clutter.

Role Product Designer
Timeline 6 Weeks
Year 2024
Tools Figma, FigJam

Overview

Remote teams were drowning in disconnected tools — tasks in one app, deadlines in another, communication scattered across channels. The result was context switching fatigue and missed deadlines.

TaskFlow brings tasks, deadlines, and team communication into one focused workspace. No tabs, no switching, no lost context.

The Problem

  • Tasks scattered across 5+ different tools with no single source of truth
  • Deadlines missed because notifications got buried in chat apps
  • No visibility into what teammates were working on
  • Overwhelming interfaces with features nobody used

The best project management tool is the one your team actually uses. Complexity kills adoption.

The Solution

A minimal, focused task board that shows what matters: what you're working on, what's next, and what's blocked. Smart notifications that surface at the right time. Team activity visible at a glance without being noisy.
