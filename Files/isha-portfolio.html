About

The person behind the pixels

I'm Isha — a UX/UI designer who believes that good design should feel invisible. It should guide you, not distract you. It should solve real problems for real people.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me painting, capturing sunsets, or exploring the intersection of art and technology. My creative journey started with a brush and canvas, and now continues with interfaces and interactions.

Every project I take on is an opportunity to learn something new and create something meaningful.
