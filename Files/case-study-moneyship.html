MoneyShip

Simplifying money management for small business owners through intuitive banking interfaces.

Role UX Designer
Timeline 8 Weeks
Year 2024
Tools Figma, Maze

Overview

Small business owners were struggling with complex banking interfaces that felt designed for corporations, not for them. The existing apps were cluttered, confusing, and made simple tasks like tracking expenses feel overwhelming.

MoneyShip was built to give small business owners a banking experience that actually understands their needs — quick cash flow checks, integrated invoice tracking, and clean separation between personal and business finances.

The Problem

  • Could not easily see cash flow at a glance — had to dig through multiple screens
  • Invoices were scattered across different tools and hard to track
  • Mobile banking felt like a desktop interface squeezed into a phone
  • No clear separation between personal and business finances

Small business owners don't want a "mini bank website" on their phone. They want to quickly answer one question: "Do I have enough to make it through this week?"

The Solution

A mobile-first banking experience built around a single, always-visible cash flow indicator. Invoice tracking integrated directly. Transactions auto-categorized with one-tap override. Clean, focused, no visual clutter.

Key Screens

The main interface revolves around three core views that work together to give business owners full control.

Invoice Tracking

Invoices auto-organized by status — paid, pending, overdue. No more digging through email.
