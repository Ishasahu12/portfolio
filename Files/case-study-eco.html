Eco Tracker

Making sustainability tracking delightful for conscious consumers who want to reduce their footprint.

Role UX Designer
Timeline 10 Weeks
Year 2023
Team Solo Project

Overview

People wanted to live more sustainably, but tracking their environmental impact felt like homework. Existing tools were guilt-driven, data-heavy, and visually boring. Making eco-friendly choices needed to feel rewarding, not punishing.

Eco Tracker was designed to turn sustainability into a progress journal — celebrating small wins instead of shaming failures.

The Problem

  • Users felt guilty rather than empowered when checking their impact
  • Data overload — tons of metrics but no clear action items
  • No sense of progress or achievement for sustainable choices
  • The app was opened once a month at best, then ignored

Sustainability should feel like a progress journal, not a report card. When people see their choices as positive contributions rather than failures to avoid, they're more likely to stick with it.

The Solution

A reward-based tracking system that celebrates small wins. Visual progress arcs, weekly challenges, and community comparison that inspires rather than shames.
